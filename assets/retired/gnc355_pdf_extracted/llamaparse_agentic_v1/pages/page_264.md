**Hazard Awareness**

### Motion Vectors

**FEATURE LIMITATIONS**
- Motion vectors display on the Traffic page only

A motion vector is a line extending from the nose of an intruder icon. Its orientation represents the intruder's direction and movement.

A yellow vector indicates when traffic meets intruding TA criteria (i.e., closing rate, distance, vertical separation).

----

#### MOTION VECTOR TYPES

| \*\*Absolute\*\* | • White vector<br/>• Depicts intruder ground track<br/>• Calculations based on intruder direction and ground speed<br/>• Endpoint depicts intruder's position over the ground at the end of the selected duration<br/>• Airborne and ground functionality                                                                                                                  |
| ---------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| \*\*Relative\*\* | • Green vector<br/>• Depicts intruder movement relative to the ownship<br/>• Calculations based on track and ground speed of both intruder and ownship<br/>• Endpoint depicts intruder's location relative to the ownship at the end of the selected duration<br/>• Airborne functionality only<br/>• "Relative Motion - Unavailable" annunciates during ground operations |
