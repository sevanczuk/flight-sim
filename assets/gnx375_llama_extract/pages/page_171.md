# USER

Lists up to 1,000 user-defined waypoints.

# SEARCH BY NAME

![Screenshot of Search Facility Name interface showing results for MCNARY including KSLE and ISLE airports with distance and bearing.](page_171_image_1_v2.jpg)

Lists all airports, NDBs, and VORs associated with the specified facility name.

Tap **Search Facility Name** to begin search.

# SEARCH BY CITY

![Screenshot of Search City Name interface showing results for SALEM including VOSM and 7M9 with distance and bearing.](page_171_image_2_v2.jpg)

Lists all airports, NDBs, and VORs found in proximity of the city.

Tap **Search City Name** to begin search.
