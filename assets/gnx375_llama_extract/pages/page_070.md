## MULTIPLE FREQUENCIES

![Multiple FREQ button](page_70_image_2_v2.jpg)

This key appears when more than one frequency is available at the indicated identifier.

Applicable to functions displaying information only (Nearest Airports, FSS, and ARTCC).

![Screenshot of a Frequencies pop-up menu showing multiple frequency options: ATIS 124.55, ASOS 124.55, Ground 121.90, Tower 119.10, Unicom 122.95, Center 125.80, and FSS 122.60.](page_70_image_1_v2.jpg)

Tap **Multiple FREQ** and select a frequency from associated pop-up.
