# ADD WAYPOINT TO AN EXISTING LEG

![Map showing an existing flight plan leg between KPDX and KEUG](page_130_image_3_v2.jpg)

You can edit, add, or omit flight plan legs by tapping or dragging your finger directly on the map.

Existing Leg

Selected Waypoint

![Map interface showing a selected waypoint KSLE and the Graphical Edit button](page_130_image_4_v2.jpg)

1. Tap any location on the map.

2. Tap **Graphical Edit**.

![Map showing the process of dragging a flight plan leg to a new waypoint](page_130_image_1_v2.jpg)

3. Tap and drag the leg to a new waypoint or airway, then release.

The temporary flight plan adjusts to show the new route.

![Map showing the updated flight plan route KPDX -> KSLE -> KEUG](page_130_image_2_v2.jpg)

If no other edits are necessary, tap **Done**.

![Done button on the touchscreen interface](page_130_image_5_v2.jpg)
