# Aural Alerts

**FEATURE LIMITATIONS**

* GNX 375 only (traffic alerts)<sup>1</sup>

* Mute alert function is applicable only to the active aural alert (does not mute future alerts)

Traffic alerts are accompanied by an aural voice message. Voice gender is configured during installation.

![Mute Alert button](page_101_image_1_v2.jpg)

Tapping **Mute Alert** silences the active traffic alert voice message.

<sup>1</sup> Aural alerts are available for GPS 175/GNC 355 systems interfaced to a traffic system (GDL 88, GTX 345, or GNX 375). They are provided directly from the traffic system LRU to the audio panel.
